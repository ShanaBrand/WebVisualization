Wind Speed (mph) at Latitudes approaching the Equator

  • Cities at Zero(0) Latitude indicates close proximity to the equator
  • Cities with Positive(+) Latitude are NORTH of the equator
  • Cities with Negative (-) Latitude are SOUTH of the Equator
On January 1, 2017 cities NORTH of the Equator experienced the highest wind speeds. Cities SOUTH of the Equaor experienced the lowest wind speeds.
